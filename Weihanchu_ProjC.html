User Instructions:
1.Use Up/Down/Right/Left keys to move the camera up/down/right/left
2.Use W/S/D/A keys to move the camera up/down/right/left
3.Use space to change light
4.Use p to change shading methods
5.Use q,e to switch light
6.Use z/x/c/v/b/n/j/k/l to change the R,G,B value for ambient,diffuse,specular
