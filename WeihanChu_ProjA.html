Please use a browser that supports "canvas"


User Instructions:
when you ran this project. Please first tap any key on the keyboard twice to move the canopy away
otherwise you will not see all the windmills
1.click ++ccw to change speed, click run/stop to run/stop, click --ccw to change speed,
2.drag the mouse in canvas to move the objects
3.click the mouse in the canvas to move the objects in a certain direction
4.press up-arrow to move objects up, press down-arrow to move objects down
5.press left-arrow to move objects left, press right-arrow to move objects right
6.press onther key to move the canopy away
